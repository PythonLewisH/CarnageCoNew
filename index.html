About

Valentin Renard

With a background in trades, I started fixing boards for friends a while ago and after a work injury kept me out of the water for a few months last year. It was the perfect activity to keep me busy during recovery and hone my skills.

I can provide for the usual repairs for dings and punctures, finbox salvation or replacement, and even colour match for seamless repairs (in some cases). Send me a message with a few pics and I'll book you in asap, my turnover goes from 1 to 2 weeks depending on the job.
